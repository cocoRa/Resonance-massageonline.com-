Your Visit

At Resonance we offer chair and table massages; both of which are performed while you are fully clothed in a common treatment room. We believe this style of clinic offers a safe and comforting place for our clients to relax communally, stimulating an atmosphere of shared “healing energy”. This form of collective stillness is a rare and precious thing in our rushed society. We strive to offer top quality massage at an affordable price right when you need it - without the hassle of booking an appointment in advance.

Your First Visit

On your first visit you will be asked to fill out our simple intake form, giving us an idea of indications/contraindications you may have for massage. We guarantee your privacy and do not share your information with any other party.

Our Massage Chairs

Our EarthLite Avila® massage chairs are ergonomically designed to allow for topmost comfort and stability, and adjust in numerous ways: your therapist will raise/lower/tilt any of the chair’s many aspects to find the perfect fit for you before beginning your massage. If you’ve ever experienced a seated massage in a lesser chair at a street festival or on-site at your workplace, it might not have left a very good impression. You can be sure that any negative chair massage preconceptions will be swept away after trying ours.

Chair or Table?

When deciding whether you would like to work on the chair or table, you may want to consider what areas you need your massage to focus on. The chair can be very effective for treatment focusing on the upper body, and provides excellent leverage for working the shoulders and arms. The table is best if you are looking for bodywork to primarily address the low back, legs and/or feet. You can view our price menu here

Our Therapists

Each Therapist in our team is required to complete extensive professional training in the anatomy and functions of the human body as well as basic massage modalities, and must adhere to a code of ethics. We employ only multi-skilled, fully credentialed Massage Therapists.

Massage modalities in which each of our therapists are skilled in include: Swedish, Relaxation, Deep Tissue, Neuromuscular Trigger Point Therapy, Hot Stone Massage, and Assisted Stretching. Each of these modalities may be administered through modified gentle or firm pressure, applied with various massage “strokes” including: Compression, Kneading, Squeezing, Friction, and Tapotement (Percussion). Feel free to let your Massage Therapist know if you are uncomfortable with any of these techniques, and would rather they not use it during your session. You can read about each individual Therapist’s specific approach to massage, and any distinctive modalities and styles they may practice, below.

Prices

Single Session

Single session massages range from 10 minutes to 1 hour. You may extend your massage beyond 1 hour if no other clients are waiting. You can always start with a shorter massage and extend it if you’d like. Any chair massage can be upgraded to a table massage for an additional $5. You may want to consider purchasing a block of minutes (Massage Package), or enrolling in our no-commitment monthly Membership for more frequent massages.

Massage Packages

Packages come in a number of minutes that you break down as you see fit. For example, opting for a 90 minute package can grant you a 20 minute chair session, 45 minutes on the table, and another 20 to spare. Package minutes can be shared with friends. or family, and never expire.

Rub Club Membership

For guests who are averaging at least 60 minutes of massage a month, Membership is a great option. Choose your level for a one time $15 start-up fee, and get new minutes (and a new Members’ Special) on the 1st of every month. Members earn reward points every time they get a massage, and can redeem them for free minutes. Memberships can be frozen at no charge, and there are no cancellation fees. If you don’t use all of your minutes in a month, they roll over to the next month, and as with Packages, can be shared with friends or family and never expire.

Membership Packages

If you find that you’ve run out of Membership minutes (but haven’t run out of reasons to get a massage) you may want to purchase a Membership Package. This package option is offered exclusively to our Rub Club Members, and is offered at 10% off the price of a regular package.
